- I'm a postdoctoral research scholar at the [International Centre for Theoretical
Sciences](https://www.icts.res.in), Bengaluru, India. I work on different
aspects of gravitational-wave (GW) physics including but not limited to study of
evolution of compact binaries using higher modes of GW, improving early-Warning
of GW using higher modes of GW, Test of General Relativity using GW and Modelling of eccentric GW waveforms. 
- Currently, I'm a member of the [LIGO Scientific Collaboration](https://www.ligo.org/)(LSC) and the LIGO-India Scientific Collaboration(LISC). I am member of the [SXS Collaboration](https://www.black-holes.org/). To know more about the activity of our group visit [Astrophysical Relativity Group](https://www.icts.res.in/research/astrorel). 
- Previously, I was a graduate student at [Harish-Chandra Research Institute](http://www.hri.res.in) where I worked mainly on the stability of stationary black hole accretion solution and the emergence of the so-called analogue gravity phenomenon in such a system.
